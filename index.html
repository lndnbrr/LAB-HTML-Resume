Professional Experience Resume - Landon Borrego

Work

Beaumont Behavioral Health

October 2023 - Present

At Beaumont Behavioral Health, I have the position labeled 'Mental Health Technician'. Being a Mental Health Technician requires an individual to consistently log patients' locations and actions every fifteen minutes to ensure their health and safety as well as others safety. Being a Mental Health Technician also requires constant and clear communication with fellow team members (mental health technicians and overseeing nurses alike) resulting in proper monitoring of patients.

Ronald McDonald House Charities - Detroit

August 2022 - December 2023

At Ronald McDonald House Charities in Detroit, I was an Associate Board Member first and then I also picked up the role as their front desk receptionist. My position as an Associate Board Member was The Head of Volunteering and Fundraising, where I would verify all members required volunteer hours and funds raised. As the Head of Volunteering, I also assisted with organizing opportunities inside and outside of the charity house for interested volunteers of the public. Lastly, as Head of Fundraising, I planned and organized fundraising events where proceeds go towards crucial aspects of the house. As a receptionist, I performed standard duties such as check-in the caregivers for children in the neighboring hospitals, guide individuals to the sources they were seeking and maintain the quality of the house as a whole.
